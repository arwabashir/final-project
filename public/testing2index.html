About fleur

Our team, snowlily, wanted to create fleur because of the increasing popularity of Asian makeup on social media. We noticed that there weren’t a lot of blog-like websites that covered all the realms of Asian beauty that weren’t heavily sponsored. Oftentimes, there would be blogs for Korean beauty, however we couldn’t find sites that showcased trends in Chinese and Japanese beauty. As enthusiastic makeup lovers, snowlily saw this as an opportunity to showcase our artistic skills, and passion for beauty in this informative website. We wanted to bring attention to these beauty trends and create a “one-stop” site to access information on how to recreate these looks, hoping to make it a little more convenient for novice Asian beauty fans to start their makeup journey.

Meet the snowlily team

Kiana Chang

Hello, I'm Kiana! As a young girl, I always sneaked into my mother's room to play with her makeup which later increased my interest in beauty. As I grew older, I wanted to learn beauty techniques that would suit my Asian features. I learned different looks that enhanced how I looked like rather than try to change my asian features. Fleur's purpose is to bring more exposure about Asian beauty techniques to everyone, so I hope you all can learn something new!
