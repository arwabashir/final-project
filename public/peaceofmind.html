About Us

Our Mission


Since our establishment in 2015, we have been dedicated to profoundly impacting lives by fostering genuine connections and offering unwaveringly compassionate care. With the opening of our doors, we embarked on a journey fueled by the desire to redefine the landscape of personal care services. Through our unwavering commitment, we strive to lead with an unparalleled standard of excellence, continuously setting new benchmarks within the industry.

Our vision extends beyond the conventional boundaries of care provision. We are not content with merely meeting expectations; we aim to surpass them. Innovation is at the core of our approach, driving us to pioneer new methods and techniques that elevate the quality of care we deliver. Our relentless pursuit of improvement is underpinned by a deep-seated passion to make a tangible and lasting difference in the lives of those we serve. Every interaction, every gesture of kindness, is imbued with the sincerity of our commitment. As we continue on our journey, our dedication remains steadfast.

Meet the Owner

Yia Chang

Meet the heart and soul behind our exceptional healthcare venture, Yia Chang, a dedicated and visionary healthcare entrepreneur whose unwavering passion lies in serving others with utmost dedication. With a profound dedication to healthcare entrepreneurship, Yia embodies a passion for serving others that is truly inspiring. Her mission is clear: to deliver compassionate and high-quality care to clients within the comfort of their own homes. Yia's vision is rooted in empowering individuals to maintain their independence and enhance their quality of life, all while providing peace of mind to both clients and their families. As the founder and guiding light of our organization, she brings a wealth of expertise and a genuine commitment to making a positive impact in the lives of those we serve.


"At the heart of our mission is a deep-seated belief in the transformative power of care. It is my privilege to lead our team in providing exceptional service that not only meets but exceeds the expectations of our clients, fostering a community of wellness, independence, and dignity."




Meet the Head Administrator/Nurse

Mee Vue

Bringing nearly two decades of dedicated service as a Registered Nurse, with a significant focus of 14 years devoted to the intricate realm of Homecare, Mee Vue is deeply driven by the ethos of holistic care. Her passion transcends the mere provision of medical assistance; it encompasses a commitment to understanding and addressing the diverse needs of her clients on every level. Mee's journey in Homecare has been defined by a relentless pursuit of excellence, where she strives to uplift the lives of those under her care by nurturing their physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. As Head Administrator/Nurse, she stands as a staunch advocate for the highest standards of quality, ensuring that each individual she serves receives personalized attention and support tailored to their unique circumstances. With unwavering dedication, Mee endeavors to forge meaningful connections with her clients, fostering an environment of trust, respect, and compassion that forms the cornerstone of our practice.


"It is my privilege to lead by example, championing the values of empathy, integrity, and excellence in all aspects of our Homecare services."
